Short Bio

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree in Artificial Intelligence from the School of Computer Science, Wuhan University. I am also a research intern at JD Explore Academy. I have authored or co-authored several papers at top conferences and international journals, including IEEE TKDE, EMNLP, COLING and etc. I won the general language understanding (GLUE) and more difficult language understanding (SuperGLUE) challenges.
